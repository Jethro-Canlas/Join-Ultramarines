You can join from anywhere in the Ultramar System. You only need to speak to any marine you encounter and pass their assessment. Once joined, you will undergo rigorous training and undergo surgery to be implanted with the gene-seed. Being a space marine will be challenging, but you will ascend as an angel of the Emperor of Man and become on of His greatest servants.
