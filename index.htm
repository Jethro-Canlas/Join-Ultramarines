Who are the Ultramarines?

The Ultramarines were part of the original 20 space marines legions created by the Emperor. We were the 12th lead by our primarch Roboute Guilliman. Hailing from our home world of Macragge, the Ultramarines are one of the most well known and powerful chapters today. Now lead by chapter master Marneus Calgar, the Ultramarines are famed for operations such as the first Tyrannic War, The Indomitus Crusade, the War of the Beasts, the Plague wars and many more.
